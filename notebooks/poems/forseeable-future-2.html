Forseeable Future - 2
Humanity awaits... 
as scientist and engineers
dare imaginations
 
Robots with artificial intelligence
will be our cooks, nurse and surgeons
 
chips scattered around us
taking data-driven decisions
 
smart clothes, homes and cities
silently fulfilling our wishes
 
saving our time and lives 
as the car itself drives
 
super-fast drone deliveries
dropping first-aids and packages
 
magical experiences with holograms and VR
Immersing in illusions as Avatars
 
But alas, every rose has thorns
it's time to set new norms
 
Re-skilling ourselves to be relevant 
as AI will be our job replacement
 
safeguarding our society
as AI can be deadly
 
data privacy and cyber security
will always be a priority
 
Humanity prepares...
as scientist and engineers
dare imaginations!
