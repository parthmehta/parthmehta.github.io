Forseeable Future -2

Humanity awaits...
as scientist and engineers
dare imaginations
 
Power of personal genomics
preventing and curing diseases
 
Designing the best genes
giving us super-human gifts
 
Growing our tissues and organs
to replace the damaged ones
 
Life will be young, long & healthy
after undergoing anti-aging therapy
 
Ambition to feed the billions
with GM plants grown in vertical farms
 
Delicious healthy meat grown in labs
animal slaughtering ends
 
Chips extending the power of your brain
to control things and communicate
 
But alas, every rose has thorns
it's time to set new norms
 
Along with new biotech revolutions
come environmental, ethical and social problems
 
Ensuring new treatments under insurance coverage
else, poor die young and rich refuse to age
 
As the retirement age increases
young may not get more opportunities
 
Long lifespans with overgrowing population
will need family planning education
 
Securing ourselves from biological weapons
doing research and publications in restricted manner
 
Humanity prepares...
as scientist and engineers
dare imaginations!
